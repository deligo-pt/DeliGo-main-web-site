Become a DeliGo Franchise Partner

Bring DeliGo to your city and grow with one of Portugal’s most dynamic mobility and delivery brands. Operate locally, backed by our technology, marketing, and support network.

Join the DeliGo Franchise Program

Become an exclusive DeliGo partner in your city or region. Manage ride-sharing, delivery, and market operations under our trusted brand — supported by DeliGo’s systems, training, and proven business model.

Why Partner with DeliGo?

Operate under the established DeliGo name with national recognition.

High ROI

Benefit from multiple revenue streams — rides, food, groceries, and courier services.

Tech & Tools

Full Support

Franchise Opportunities

DeliGo Ride

Manage ride-sharing and TVDE operations in your city with DeliGo’s dispatch system and local fleet network.

DeliGo Food

Run a regional food and grocery delivery business with access to partner restaurants and vendors.

DeliGo Express

Offer parcel and same-day delivery services through local courier partnerships.

Franchise Requirements

  • Registered company or ability to form a local business entity.
  • Minimum investment capital and operational resources.
  • Knowledge of local market, regulations, and customer behavior.
  • Commitment to DeliGo standards and customer service excellence.
  • Willingness to participate in training and periodic performance reviews.

How to Apply for a DeliGo Franchise

  1. Step 1: Submit your business and location details through our online form.
  2. Step 2: Attend a meeting with our Franchise Development team.
  3. Step 3: Review and sign the franchise agreement.
  4. Step 4: Receive training and launch your local DeliGo operation.
Submit Application

Our Franchise Team will reach out within 2–3 business days after your application.
