Why become a DeliGo Courier?

Flexible Schedule

Deliver whenever it suits you — mornings, evenings, or weekends.

Instant Earnings

Earn for every completed delivery with extra bonuses during rush hours.

Smart Routes

Use the DeliGo Courier App to navigate and optimize your deliveries easily.

Safety First

We provide clear guidelines, insurance support, and courier safety training.

Courier Requirements

  • Valid ID or Passport
  • Own vehicle (motorbike, car, or bicycle) with valid papers
  • Smartphone with GPS and mobile internet
  • Basic understanding of English or Portuguese
  • Proof of address and bank account details

How to Join DeliGo Express

  1. Step 1: Complete the courier application form online.
  2. Step 2: Upload your ID, driver’s license, and vehicle documents.
  3. Step 3: Attend a short verification and onboarding call.
  4. Step 4: Get access to the DeliGo Courier App and start delivering!
