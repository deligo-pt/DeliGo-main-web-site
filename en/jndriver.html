Safety & Support

We offer insurance, in-app safety tools, and 24/7 driver support.

Driver Requirements

  • Valid driver’s license (TVDE or equivalent for Portugal)
  • Vehicle in good condition (car or van, up to 7 years old)
  • Clean criminal record and driving history
  • Smartphone with internet and GPS
  • Vehicle insurance and inspection documents (IPO)
  • Bank account for receiving payments

How to Apply

  1. Step 1: Complete the online registration form with your details.
  2. Step 2: Upload your documents — ID, license, insurance, and vehicle papers.
  3. Step 3: Complete a short verification and onboarding call.
  4. Step 4: Get activated and start accepting rides in the DeliGo Driver App!
