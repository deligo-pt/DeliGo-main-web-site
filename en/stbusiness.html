DeliGo Business Support

Get help managing your corporate account, employee rides, and business billing with DeliGo for Business.

Welcome to DeliGo Business Support

From ride programs to food and courier solutions, DeliGo Business helps companies move efficiently. This page provides all the resources and guidance you need to manage your business account.

Contact Support

Account Setup & Management

Start using DeliGo Business by setting up your organization’s account and adding your team members.

  • Sign up for a DeliGo Business account using your company details and NIF.
  • Assign an admin user to manage team access and billing.
  • Add employees via email invitation or bulk upload.
  • Define travel or spending limits for rides, food, and courier services.

Corporate Rides & Delivery Programs

Manage company rides, meetings, and logistics effortlessly through your DeliGo Business dashboard.

  • Offer business rides to employees with automatic expense tracking.
  • Enable food delivery for team lunches, events, or overtime meals.
  • Use DeliGo Courier for sending documents, parcels, or business materials.
  • Assign multiple admins to manage rides across departments or branches.

Billing, Payments & Invoicing

DeliGo Business simplifies expense management and billing for your organization.

  • Choose between monthly consolidated invoices or per-trip billing.
  • Download itemized expense reports directly from the admin dashboard.
  • Pay via bank transfer, card, or automatic billing.
  • Receive receipts and VAT-compliant invoices for all corporate expenses.

Employee Access & Control

Easily manage team permissions and usage within your DeliGo Business account.

  • Add or remove employees at any time from the admin panel.
  • Set spending or travel limits by department or employee role.
  • Assign approval workflows for ride or meal requests.
  • Export employee usage reports for HR or accounting teams.
