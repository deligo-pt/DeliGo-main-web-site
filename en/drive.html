How to get started

Step 1

Apply online

Fill out the registration form above. We’ll contact you via email or in-app to share the required document list.

  • Valid driver’s license (Portugal/EU)
  • ID or residence card
  • Vehicle documents (if you own a car)
Step 2

Upload your documents

Upload your documents through the DeliGo Driver App. Once verified, your profile will be activated.

No car? No problem

How the DeliGo Driver App works

  1. Receive nearby ride requests — accept the ones you want.
  2. Follow in-app navigation to pick up your passenger.
  3. Drive to the destination and end the trip when complete.
  4. Earn more during peak hours or in high-demand areas.
  5. Track your daily earnings and trip history directly from the app dashboard.
  6. Get instant route updates and safety alerts during every trip.
  7. Rate passengers and get rated to build your professional driver profile.
  8. Receive weekly performance bonuses and incentives for top-rated service.
