Fleet Requirements

  • Company registration & NIF (Portugal) or equivalent.
  • Valid insurance and roadworthiness certificates for all vehicles.
  • Drivers with valid licenses (TVDE or applicable category) & clean records.
  • Bank account (IBAN) for payouts and settlements.
  • Smartphone access for drivers (DeliGo Driver App).

Fleet Platform Features

Live Ops Dashboard

Track vehicles, driver status, trip progress, and hotspots in real time.

Driver Management

Invite, approve, or suspend drivers; manage schedules and compliance docs.

Earnings & Analytics

Daily/weekly revenue, utilization, cancellations, and ratings at a glance.

Reports & Exports

Download CSV/PDF statements and per-driver breakdowns for accounting.
