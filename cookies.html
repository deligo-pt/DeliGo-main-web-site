Cookies Policy

How DeliGo uses cookies and similar technologies on www.deligo.pt and our mobile apps.

1. About this policy

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and the choices you have. It applies to the website www.deligo.pt and related services operated by PIXELMIRACLE, LDA (DeliGo), Rua Joaquim Agostinho 16C, 1750-126 Lisboa, Portugal. For more details on how we process personal data, please read our Privacy Policy.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, and region) over a period of time. We may also use similar technologies like SDKs in our mobile apps, and local storage in your browser.

3. Types of cookies we use

  • Essential (Strictly Necessary) – Required for the site to function (e.g., security, session, load balancing). These cannot be switched off.
  • Performance & Analytics – Help us measure traffic, usage, and improve features (aggregated statistics).
  • Functional – Remember choices (language, theme) and enhance your experience.
  • Marketing – Used to deliver and measure ads relevant to you across websites and apps.

5. Managing your preferences

You can choose which optional cookies to accept by using our cookie preferences tool. You can also block or delete cookies through your browser or device settings. However, blocking essential cookies may impact site functionality.

Open Cookie Preferences

  • Browser controls: See help pages for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge for instructions to clear or block cookies.
  • Mobile apps: Use device settings (e.g., “Reset advertising ID” or “Limit ad tracking”).
  • Analytics opt-outs: Some analytics providers offer their own opt-out mechanisms.

6. Legal basis

Where required by law, we request your consent for non-essential cookies (e.g., analytics and marketing). Essential cookies rely on our legitimate interest to deliver secure and reliable services. You may withdraw consent at any time via the preferences tool.

7. Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy to reflect changes in technology, law, or our services. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
