Become a DeliGo Rider

Join the DeliGo delivery team and start earning with flexible hours, weekly payments, and complete freedom. Deliver food, groceries, and parcels in your city — your time, your choice.

Drive. Deliver. Earn with DeliGo.

Whether you have a motorbike, scooter, or bicycle, DeliGo lets you earn extra income by completing deliveries near you. Be your own boss and make every trip count!

Apply Now

Why become a DeliGo Rider?

Flexible Hours

Work whenever you want — part-time or full-time, your schedule is yours.

Earn More

Get paid for every delivery, plus tips and weekly bonuses during peak hours.

Work Anywhere

Deliver across your city with real-time navigation and instant order notifications.

Support & Safety

We provide app support, training, and safety tools to keep you protected.

What You’ll Need

  • Valid ID (Citizen Card or Passport)
  • Driving license (A, B, or equivalent for vehicle type)
  • Smartphone (Android or iOS) with internet access
  • Vehicle (bike, scooter, or car) and valid insurance
  • Bank account for receiving payments

How to Apply

  1. Step 1: Complete the online application form with your details.
  2. Step 2: Upload your ID, license, and insurance documents.
  3. Step 3: Attend a quick verification call or online orientation.
  4. Step 4: Once approved, log in to the DeliGo Rider app and start earning!
Start Application

Already applied? You’ll be contacted by our Rider Support team within 24–48 hours.
