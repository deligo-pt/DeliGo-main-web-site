Couriers Support

Support for DeliGo Express Couriers — registration, delivery help, earnings, and safety guidelines.

Welcome to DeliGo Couriers Support

Deliver parcels, documents, and express goods safely with DeliGo. Find answers, training tips, and contact details for courier partners below.

Contact Support

Courier Registration & Setup

To become a DeliGo Courier, you’ll need to complete a short registration and verification process:

  • Apply through the DeliGo Courier App or the website registration page.
  • Upload valid ID, vehicle papers (if applicable), and insurance proof.
  • Attend a short online orientation on handling and safety.
  • Once approved, log in and start accepting delivery requests.

How DeliGo Express Works

DeliGo Express Couriers handle small parcels, documents, and eCommerce deliveries. Here’s how the process works:

  • Accept available jobs near your location via the Courier App.
  • Pick up the package from the sender within the scheduled time.
  • Confirm delivery by collecting the receiver’s signature or photo proof.
  • Get paid instantly after successful delivery completion.

Payments & Earnings

Couriers earn based on distance, delivery type, and peak-hour bonuses. Here’s how to manage and track your income:

  • Payments are calculated per completed delivery (plus tips).
  • Withdraw funds anytime from the app’s Wallet section.
  • Weekly earnings reports are emailed automatically.
  • For payment issues, contact support with delivery ID and date.

Safety, Quality & Package Handling

DeliGo prioritizes safety for both couriers and customers. Follow these steps to ensure a safe and professional delivery:

  • Always verify package details before pickup.
  • Use insulated or waterproof bags for weather-sensitive items.
  • Never open or tamper with parcels.
  • Follow road safety rules and report any accidents immediately.
  • For high-value items, ensure signature verification at delivery.
